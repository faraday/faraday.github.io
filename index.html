Philosophy

  • Most ML problems are product and data problems in disguise.
  • Simple, well-instrumented models in production beat fancy models stuck in notebooks.
  • Owning the full loop (data → model → serving → eval) matters more than owning a narrow slice.
  • Clear metrics and honest evaluation save more time than any clever architecture.
